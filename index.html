About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate from BRAC University with a deep passion for building meaningful software that solves real problems. My journey in tech has been driven by curiosity — from crafting full-stack web applications using the MERN stack to competing in programming contests and mentoring peers. I thrive at the intersection of clean architecture and intuitive user experience, with a particular focus on secure, scalable systems.

Beyond the code, I'm a leader and community builder. As Senior Executive of the BRAC University Computer Club, I've organized technical events, led cross-functional teams, and mentored junior members — because I believe great software is built by great teams. I was recognized on the Vice Chancellor's List four times and the Dean's List four times, and placed 35th in the Intra BRAC University Programming Contest, reflecting both my academic commitment and my love for competitive problem-solving.

I'm currently seeking opportunities where I can contribute to high-impact engineering challenges, grow alongside talented teams, and help shape products used by people around the world.
